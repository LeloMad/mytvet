General Info

Here are some general things to note;

  • We currently only keep a library of papers for Electrical, Civil and Mechanical Engineering courses.

  • We’re looking to add papers for Business Studies based on your demand. So let us know if you'd like us to add them by contacting us.

  • Please be advised that all the exam papers that have been uploaded on the site, are the only papers that we have. Unfortunately, we cannot assist you with what isn’t on our site currently.

  • We update our site regularly to upload missing papers as we find them.

  • You’re welcome to email us papers we don't have on our site yet.
